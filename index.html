About This Event

Built by Trainees.
For Everyone.

Healthcare innovation is accelerating how patients are cared for, diagnosed, treated, and prevented from illness. Yet the benefits of innovation often remain concentrated in well-resourced settings — and patients with the greatest need are last to benefit.

Aligned with Albert Einstein College of Medicine's mission to advance health equity through science, education, and community engagement, the Healthcare Innovation Summit: Innovation for Everyone convenes leaders to explore how innovation can scale without leaving people behind.

EIG is a student-led, institution-facing platform that bridges medicine, business, and innovation. Through seminars, fellowships, case competitions, and incubators, we prepare the next generation of physician-innovators.
